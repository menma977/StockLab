# Privacy Policy for StockLab

**Last updated:** May 30, 2026

StockLab is an offline inventory management application. This policy explains how data is handled when you use StockLab.

## Data We Collect

**StockLab itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

All your inventory data (products, batches, ledgers, settings) is stored locally on your device and never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

StockLab uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

- Advertising ID (AAID)
- Device information (model, OS version)
- IP address
- App interaction data

This data is used solely for displaying and measuring ad performance. AdMob data collection is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using a local database. There is no cloud storage, no account registration, and no server communication beyond what is required for AdMob ad loading.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by opening StockLab, going to **Settings > Clear All Data**. This will permanently remove all local data.

If you wish to reset your Advertising ID, go to your device's **Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID**.
